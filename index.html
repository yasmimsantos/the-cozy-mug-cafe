About the café

Welcome to The Cozy Mug, a cozy haven for coffee enthusiasts and community gatherings. Nestled in the heart of the city, The Cozy Mug offers an inviting ambiance where each detail — from soft lighting to comfortable seating — has been curated to make guests feel right at home. Our carefully selected coffee beans are sourced from the finest growers worldwide, roasted to perfection, and brewed with precision by our skilled baristas.

About our space

The Cozy Mug is a flexible space that hosts events from birthday celebrations to corporate meetings, with customizable packages and AV-equipped private rooms. It also offers barista courses, covering topics like bean selection, brewing techniques, and latte art, led by experienced baristas for coffee enthusiasts to learn and enjoy.

Our menu

Americano

A shot of espresso diluted with hot water, offering a milder flavor.

Cappuccino

Similar to a latte but with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam, creating a frothy texture.

Cold Brew

Coffee brewed with cold water over an extended period (12-24 hours) for a smooth, less acidic flavor, served over ice.

Espresso

A strong, concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Flat White

A smooth, velvety coffee made with espresso and microfoam, less frothy than a cappuccino.

Latte

A shot of espresso mixed with steamed milk and topped with a small amount of foam.

Macchiato

An espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk, offering a strong coffee flavor with a touch of creaminess.

Mocha

A combination of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, often topped with whipped cream.

Cold Brew Tonic

A refreshing mix of cold brew coffee and tonic water, garnished with a slice of citrus. The effervescence of the tonic pairs perfectly with the smooth, bold flavor of cold brew.

Mojito

A twist on the classic mojito, combining fresh mint, lime, rum for a unique and energizing cocktail.

Iced Lemonade

A refreshing blend of iced lemonade.

Coffee Paloma

A refreshing cocktail made with grapefruit soda, tequila, and a splash of espresso, offering a zesty, slightly bitter flavor profile with a coffee kick.

Avocado Toast

Fresh, mashed avocado spread on toasted sourdough bread, topped with a poached egg, cherry tomatoes, and a sprinkle of chili flakes for extra flavor.

Classic Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes served with maple syrup, butter, and a choice of toppings like fresh berries, bananas, or whipped cream.

Breakfast Burrito

A warm tortilla filled with scrambled eggs, crispy bacon or sausage, cheese, and avocado, wrapped up for a hearty, satisfying breakfast.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

A layer of creamy Greek yogurt topped with granola, fresh fruits like berries or kiwi, and a drizzle of honey for a light and refreshing option.

Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad

Tender grilled chicken served on a bed of romaine lettuce, topped with homemade Caesar dressing, croutons, and shaved Parmesan cheese.

Veggie Wrap

A whole wheat wrap filled with hummus, roasted vegetables, mixed greens, avocado, and a light vinaigrette dressing for a fresh, vegetarian option.

Classic BLT Sandwich

Crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, and juicy tomatoes, served on toasted whole grain or white bread, with a smear of mayonnaise.

Turkey and Swiss Panini

Sliced turkey, Swiss cheese, and caramelized onions pressed between slices of artisan bread, grilled to perfection for a warm, melty sandwich.

Quinoa Bowl

A nutritious bowl of quinoa topped with grilled vegetables, avocado, a poached egg, and a light lemon-tahini dressing.

Minestrone Soup

A hearty Italian soup filled with fresh vegetables, beans, pasta, and a savory tomato-based broth, perfect for a lighter but comforting lunch.

Chocolate Cake

Our decadent chocolate cake is rich, moist, and layered with velvety smooth frosting, making it the perfect treat for any chocolate lover.

Tiramisu

A classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, cocoa powder, and a dusting of cocoa for a rich, creamy finish.

Lemon Meringue Pie

A tangy lemon custard filling in a buttery, flaky pie crust, topped with a fluffy, toasted meringue for the perfect balance of tart and sweet.

Berry Cheesecake

A smooth and creamy cheesecake topped with a generous layer of fresh mixed berries and a hint of lemon zest for a refreshing twist.
